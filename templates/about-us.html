About Us


Awareness and Protocols

Hello future escapers! While our Escape Rooms progresses for its completion, we would like to remind you that our MAIN priorities come to the health and safety of our employees and most importantly our customers. We are committed to following guidelines and procedures from trusted sources to help keep our facilities clean, safe, and playable for our future escapers. This means we will be putting a few mandatory rules temporarily to avoid the spread of COVID-19, and we ask for your compliance in these regulations to these rules. Get Away Games & Media goals for our future escapers is for them to have an amazing fun experience and being able to challenge themselves in our escape rooms while being in a safe and clean environment. We pray that everyone is doing good and being safe and hope to see you soon.


Enhanced procedures and Guidelines

Our top priority is to keep both our employees and our customers safe. As part of that, we are strictly adhering to the following safety measures:
-We provide private escape rooms so you can play with people within your group.
-We sternly suggest for each player to wash their hands before and after each game. Hand sanitizer and gloves will be offered at our facilities.
-Face masks or anything used for covering mouth and nose will be REQUIRED when entering our escape rooms.
-We will be sanitizing the escape rooms after each game, and paying extra attention on most commonly touched areas and surfaces with medical-grade disinfectants.
-Please arrive with your party and wait for our call to come inside.
-We ask to stay six feet apart from other groups for social distancing.
